I am a Researcher at ICAR-CNR, the Artificial Intelligence Institute of the National Research Council of Italy. I got my PhD in AI at the University of Pisa, advised by Giuseppe Manco and Francesco Bonchi.

My research topics revolve around the theme of Online Social Safety, my focus is on developing scalable computational methods to protect users and communities online.

I aim to empower citizens, platforms and public entities with tools to audit and mitigate digital threats, ensuring safer and more inclusive online spaces.
